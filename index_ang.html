About Us


For more 50 years, the Wild Circus has been traveling around the continent for you to live amazing and unforgettable shows.

Our history

Created in 1962 by the BULLE brothers, the Wild Circus saw his big top built up for the first time in England, under the name of "Bubble Circus". The circus was a real success in their country. The success was such that the troop developed and decided to cross the borders of its native country.

Tow years later, in 1964, France welcomed the Bubble Circus for their first performance abroad. The tour lasted one month for a total of 9 performances in different cities. The shows started in Paris, then went to Lille, Reims, Strasbourg, Lyon, Toulouse, Bordeaux, Orléans and finished at La Loupe. The trip was an achievement and allowed the circus increasing its popularity.

In 1994, the society Wild&Co, owner of many properties and services in France, bought at a high price Bubble Circus and renamed it "Wild Circus".

Since 2012, artists have to wear socks to do the shows, rules established by Wild&Co.
